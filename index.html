About Me

Hi, I'm Marcos Jiménez Saz, a passionate videogame programmer with a focus on creating interactive and exciting experiences. I graduated in videogame programming at ESAT, where I developed a solid foundation in videogame programming and design. Throughout my training, I have worked on several projects, using tools such as Unity and Unreal Engine, and programming languages such as C, C++ and C#.

I participated in a technology and videogame congress called OWN (formerly DreamHack) in Valencia, where I had the opportunity to present one of my projects in a booth and later publish it on the Steam platform for free, sharing my ideas and learning from other professionals in the industry. This experience reinforced my commitment to keep learning and growing. I'm excited for the opportunity to contribute to innovative projects and collaborate with others to create games that leave an impression on players.

I am currently training through courses on platforms like Udemy to improve my skills and keep up to date with the latest trends in videogame development.
