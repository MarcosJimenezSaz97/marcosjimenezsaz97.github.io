About Me

I am a videogame programmer graduated from ESAT, with experience in developing projects published on platforms like Steam, using tools like Unreal Engine and Unity. During my training, I presented a project at a technology and videogame congress in Valencia called DreamHack / OWN, excelling in both technical skills and teamwork. Passionate about video game development, I continue to train on platforms like Udemy to keep up to date with the latest technologies and bring innovative solutions to the sector.
